Origins of Lua

Lua was created in 1993 at the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, by Roberto Ierusalimschy, Luiz Henrique de Figueiredo, and Waldemar Celes. It was developed to provide a lightweight, flexible scripting language for embedded systems.

Development & Evolution

Over time, Lua evolved to support features such as extensible semantics, first-class functions, and an efficient interpreter. Today, Lua remains a popular choice for game development, embedded applications, and general-purpose scripting.
